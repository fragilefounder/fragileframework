What even is frAGILE?

frAGILE is a parody framework for product teams who have seen some things. It’s for anyone who’s sat through a four-hour retro that produced one sticky note and a migraine. For those who watched “Agile” get implemented like a corporate trust fall—and hit the floor. And for the folks doing real work between the alignment meetings about alignment meetings.

“We’re not even agile.”

If you’ve ever typed that into Slack mid-sprint, you’re home.

Is this serious?

Sort of. Not really. Maybe a little.

frAGILE is satire. But it’s satire with insight. We laugh because it’s true. And because if we didn’t laugh, we’d probably cry.

Everything here is exaggerated. But behind the jokes are real patterns, real frustrations, and real tips. This is for product people who want to feel seen, reflect on the chaos, and maybe fix one thing at a time.
