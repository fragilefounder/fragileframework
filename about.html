👋 Who I Am

I’m Thach Nguyen — Director of Product Management by day, and creator of frAGILE Framework™ by late-night, slightly sarcastic, caffeine-fueled sessions.

I’ve led product teams across startups and Fortune 500s in the title, lending, and real estate tech industries. Today, I support an amazing team of wickedly smart product minds. We are building a modern title production platform. We are also building strong, clear systems that will thrive with or without me.

🎯 Why frAGILE?

frAGILE started as a joke. Like most good jokes, it came from truth. I’ve sat through retros that resolved nothing, roadmaps that solved everything (on paper), and daily standups that stood for absolutely no progress.

I built frAGILE to expose the chaos, celebrate the survivors, and offer a little clarity in the form of satire. Because sometimes the best way to move forward... is to laugh, reset, and keep going.
