
frAGILE PDF Style Guidelines
----------------------------

Use this for all PDFs: Manifesto, Playbook, etc.

COLORS:
- Header/Title: Dark Blue (#1E3A8A)
- Accent/CTA: Orange (#F97316)
- Background: Off-White (#F5EEE3)
- Body Text: Dark Brown (#2C1C0C)

FONTS:
- Header: Montserrat Bold (36pt, all caps or Title Case)
- Subheaders: Montserrat SemiBold (24pt)
- Body Text: Roboto Regular (14pt)

LAYOUT:
- Use wide margins and lots of whitespace.
- Icons or illustrations should be minimalist, not clip-art.
- Include footer with “© 2025 frAGILE Framework™ | fragileframework.com”

CANVA TEMPLATE:
- Create a 3-column layout template
- Top bar: logo left, page title center, empty right
- Middle: main content with large typography and quotes
- Bottom: color block for CTAs (“Read more at fragileframework.com”)

TONE:
- Satirical but clean
- Avoid low-res jokes or overly goofy fonts
- Humor comes from content, not cartoon visuals
