Why frAGILE?

  • 🧠 Think like a Product Manager, but without the burden of outcomes.
  • 📈 Measure everything, especially story points. Especially your team’s feelings.
  • 🛠️ Ship Fast. Break Faster. Velocity is everything. Quality is optional.
  • 📆 Daily Standups, where status is vague and blockers are emotional.
  • 🏗️ Build the MVP, and keep MVP-ing until the M becomes meaningless.

The frAGILE Manifesto

  • Individuals and interactions over results and accountability
  • Comprehensive documentation over working software
  • Responding to change over delivering anything concrete
  • Following the roadmap, unless a stakeholder sneezes

Roles We Support

  • Chief Alignment Officer (CAO): Knows nothing, attends everything.
  • Scrum Grandmaster: Only speaks in rituals.
  • Senior VP of Gantt Charts: Waterfall in disguise. Wears a cape.
  • The Agile Whisperer: Says “velocity” and “empowerment” every third word.
